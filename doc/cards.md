### Attack Range Formula

```
Effective range(A вҶ’ B) = seat distance(A, B) вҲ’ A's (+1) horse bonus + B's (вҲ’1) horse bonus
```
A can attack B only if effective range вүӨ weapon range (default 1 with no weapon).
